GIF Exercise Guide

See the movement before you train.

Use these GIFs as simple visual cues. You do not need perfect form knowledge before starting. Move slowly and stay controlled.

Upper Body

Push-Up

Keep your body in one line. Do not let the hips drop.

Upper Body

Knee Push-Up

Use this when regular push-ups are too hard.

Upper Body

Pike Push-Up

Keep the hips high and move with control.

Upper Body

Diamond Push-Up

Keep the hands narrow and avoid flaring the elbows too much.

Lower Body

Squat

Sit the hips down and keep the chest steady.

Lower Body

Wide Squat

Use a wider stance and stay controlled through the legs.

Lower Body

Split Squat

Move slowly and keep balance on both sides.

Back

Superman

Lift gently. Do not throw the body upward.

Back

Back Extension

Use the upper back and avoid over-arching.

Back

Front-Arm Push-Up

Keep wrists comfortable and control the press.

Core

Crunch

Lift with the abs. Do not pull the neck.

Core

Leg Raise

Move slowly and keep the lower back controlled.

Core

Cycling Crunch

Rotate with control. Do not rush the reps.
