Week 1 Mission

Learn the flow.

Do not chase perfect workouts. Learn the upper, lower, back, repeat, rest rhythm.

Week 1 Progress0 / 7 marked
Day 1 - Upper BodyAbout 15 min
  • Knee Push-Up - 8 reps x 2
  • Pike Push-Up - 5 reps x 2
  • Diamond Push-Up - 4 reps x 2
  • Crunch - 12 reps x 2

Rest: 60 sec

Point: Learn the upper body day without rushing.

Day 2 - Lower BodyAbout 15 min
  • Squat - 12 reps x 2
  • Wide Squat - 10 reps x 2
  • Split Squat - 6 each side x 2
  • Leg Raise - 10 reps x 2

Rest: 60 sec

Point: Stay stable before going deeper.

Day 3 - BackAbout 15 min
  • Superman - 12 reps x 2
  • Back Extension - 10 reps x 2
  • Front-Arm Push-Up - 5 reps x 2
  • Cycling Crunch - 10 each side x 2

Rest: 60 sec

Point: Move with control. Do not swing.

Day 4 - Upper BodyAbout 15 min
  • Knee Push-Up - 8-10 reps x 2
  • Pike Push-Up - 5 reps x 2
  • Diamond Push-Up - 5 reps x 2
  • Crunch - 15 reps x 2

Rest: 60 sec

Point: Feel steadier than Day 1.

Day 5 - Lower BodyAbout 15 min
  • Squat - 12 reps x 2
  • Wide Squat - 10 reps x 2
  • Split Squat - 8 each side x 2
  • Leg Raise - 10 reps x 2

Rest: 60 sec

Point: Notice if one side feels less stable.

Day 6 - BackAbout 15 min
  • Superman - 12 reps x 2
  • Back Extension - 10 reps x 2
  • Front-Arm Push-Up - 6 reps x 2
  • Cycling Crunch - 12 each side x 2

Rest: 60 sec

Point: Keep your wrists comfortable.

Day 7 - Rest5-10 min
  • Walk for 5-10 minutes
  • Light stretching

Rest: No sets today

Point: Rest is part of the plan.
